About Me

I'm Chewbacca, a 200-year-old Wookiee. I become Han Solo's companion after Han, an Imperial officer at the time, refused an order to kill him, for which Han was dismissed from the Imperial Navy and became a smuggler. To repay my debt, I protected Han for the rest of his life, and served as his co-pilot on the Millennium Falcon.

I'm 2.28m tall, I'm covered with long hair, and I wear only a bandolier. My weapon of choice is the Wookiee bowcaster (a crossbow-shaped directed-energy weapon).
